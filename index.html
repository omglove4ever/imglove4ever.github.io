Grímseying Solstice Festival

Event Details

Date: June 20 - June 23, 2024

Location: Grímsey, Akureyri

Timing

June 20 6:00 PM - June 23 9:00 PM

Introduction

At Sólstaðir, the Grímseyers celebrate "the nightless springtime world, where the wide view shines", as it says in the poem by Stephan G. This year, the program of the festival is, as usual, rather grand and everything is covered. In connection with the Solstice Festival, the famous artist Bubbi Morthens comes to Grímsey and, among other things, moves the artwork "Orbis et Globus" so that it sits in the correct location of the Arctic Circle in 2024. Everyone is welcome to Grímsey. Accommodation can be reserved and there is always room at the campsite.
